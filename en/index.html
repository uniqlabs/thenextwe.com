Topics

You can tackle a diverse range of problems in your company with TheNextWe business coaching. All our programs are tailor-made. Common issues are:

Mastering digitalization

Digitalization is far more than the mere electronification of products and processes. It demands new ways of thinking and working. But the concerns of team members are significant. We resolve these concerns so that staff on all levels of hierarchy can shape the change proactively and with enthusiasm.

Growing sales

When sales targets aren't met, this is often due to concerns about the customer and doubts about the product. The quickest way to grow revenue is to resolve these doubts and implement the new sales behavior in daily business.

Reducing costs

Cost pressure in one area often causes problems in other areas. We identify the root cause and resolve thinking patterns that lead to this situation. We thereby facilitate new solutions and enable enthusiastic implementation and new results within a short timespan.

Employee development

Whether a high potential is facing a new challenge or a seasoned executive wants to get to the next level - our leadership coaching program lets everyone realize their potential. At Procter&Gamble we enable managers to reach personal development goals.

Method

TheNextWe offers business coaching to solve fundamental company problems. Our coaching method combines three distinct disciplines: Management, psychology and technology.

Insa achieves growth by changing mindsets

Insa Klasing

While Insa was CEO of KFC Germany, the restaurant chain opened almost as many new locations within 5 years as it had done in 40 years before. This became possible by resolving the concerns of all stakeholders. The best strategy is useless if the people involved are not convinced by it and will thus not implement it. Coaching is the most effective way to resolve such concerns. For the first time, TheNextWe makes coaching available for many users simultaneously and thereby enables collective mindset change and large-scale transformation.

Anke brings 25 years of experience in cognitive transformation

The team grew with the arrival of cognitive psychologist Anke Kaupp. Anke has more than 25 years of experience as a cognitive coach and has enabled thousands of people to realize their true potential.
