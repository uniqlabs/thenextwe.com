Topics

With TheNextWe® business coaching you can tackle a diverse range of problems in your company. All our programs are tailor-made. Common issues are:

Mastering digitalization

Digitalization is far more than the mere electronification of products and processes. It demands new ways of thinking and working. But the concerns of team members are significant. We resolve these concerns so that staff on all levels of hierarchy can shape the change proactively and with enthusiasm.

Customer centricity

In a digital world, established sales strategies no longer work. Sales staff need to fundamentally rethink their identity and the way they interact with customers. We support them to rid themselves of old product-centric thinking and enable them to put the customer front and center in everything they do.

Team integration

We ignite a colloborative spirit in newly formed teams so that they enjoy working together successfully. Whether it's marketing and IT joining forces for an innovation project or two companies coming together in a merger - we resolve reservations on both sides and strengthen collaboration.

Female Leadership

We enhance confidence of female leaders so they don't sell themselves short. We work with them on their thinking to improve visibility and conflict resilience, so that they can use conflicts constructively instead of avoiding them.

Method

TheNextWe® offers business coaching to solve fundamental business problems. Our coaching method combines three distinct disciplines: Management, psychology and technology.

Insa achieves growth by changing mindsets

Insa Klasing

While Insa was CEO of KFC Germany, the restaurant chain opened almost as many new locations within 5 years as it had done in 40 years before. This became possible by first resolving the concerns of all stakeholders. The best strategy is useless if the people involved are not convinced by it and will thus not implement it. Coaching is the most effective way to resolve such concerns. For the first time, TheNextWe makes coaching available for many users simultaneously and thereby enables collective mindset change and large-scale transformation.

Anke brings 25 years of experience in cognitive transformation

Anke Kaupp

Anke has more than 25 years of experience as a cognitive psychologist and has enabled thousands of people to realize their true potential as a business coach. She studied cognitive methods with Prof. Albert Ellis in New York, among others. These methods are especially efficient and lead to sustainable results. They are based on the realization that only a new mindset can enable new behavior, which in turn enables new results. At TheNextWe, Anke adapted these methods for the achievement of business goals.
